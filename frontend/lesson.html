🌍 Our Mission

Education Has No Borders

Millions of African students lack access to quality science education and rarely encounter space science or advanced technology training. KIZORA exists to change that — by bringing world-class, AI-powered education to every student, in every corner of Africa.

By 2035, we aim to train 1 million students and contribute to Africa's role in the future of space technology.
