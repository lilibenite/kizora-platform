# KIZORA Vision

KIZORA – Institute of Space & Technology

Founded by TWAGIRAYO Lili Benite

## Vision

KIZORA aims to build a global platform that empowers students with knowledge in science, technology, artificial intelligence, and space exploration.

The mission is to make advanced scientific learning accessible to students around the world, especially in Africa.

KIZORA will combine education, innovation, and space technology to inspire the next generation of scientists and engineers.
